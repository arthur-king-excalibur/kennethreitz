Why Ruby Scares Me

Sun 20 December 2009
By Kenneth Reitz

In Code.

Ruby scares me. It's not the language that strikes fear in my heart, however; it is the community. ## The Reasons 1. They treat Ruby as the Messiah — there is nothing better. There is nothing else. Only Ruby. 2. Community Leader Idol Wordship — did anyone catch the (online presence) death of _why? 3. "Now we can finally get things done". — See next question. 4. Have you ever seen Ruby code? Look at below. 5. See Reason # 4. def remember(&a_block) @block = a_block end

remember {|name| puts "Hello, #{name}!"}

@block.call("Jon")
# => "Hello, Jon!"

Can you really tell me, with a degree of certainty, what that's supposed to do? Please, just shoot me now.
