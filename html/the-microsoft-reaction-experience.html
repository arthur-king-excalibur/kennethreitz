The Microsoft Reaction Experience

Thu 16 July 2009
By Kenneth Reitz

In Code.

It seems like Microsoft base its entire product line nowadays on reactions.

They will be apparently be opening Microsoft Retailer stores across the nation soon. Why? I mean, I know they have the Zune, the XBox, and, well, Windows. But why a store? It works great for Apple – But Apple and Microsoft are two very different companies. Nowadays, Apple is an innovator. Microsoft is a copy-cat essentially. A few things are excluded from this of course (Sharepoint), but in general, the company just isn't all that Creative. I mean Silverlight? Flash has been out for well over 8 years. Why even try to compete? Sure Flash is a ridiculous resource hog and it's great that you want to make something better. But Microsoft: please, for once, try to make something NEW for a change?
